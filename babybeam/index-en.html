BabyBeam

Turn two iPhones or iPads into a private video baby monitor.

  • Truly privateVideo and audio go directly phone-to-phone, end-to-end encrypted. Nothing is recorded or stored.
  • No hassleNo account, no subscription, no ads. Pair with a single code or a QR scan — grandparents manage it too.
  • Smart alertsTells real crying from other sounds (on-device), alerts your lock screen, and shows the baby phone's battery and status.
  • Ready for the nightNight vision, a dimmed screen, remote white noise and lullabies, and a listen-only mode.
