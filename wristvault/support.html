Common questions

Does my collection sync between devices?
Yes — if you're signed in to iCloud, your collection syncs across your own Apple devices automatically. It stays in your private iCloud; no one else can see it. You can check the status under Settings → iCloud.
Is my data private?
Completely. There's no account, no tracking, and no analytics. Your data never leaves your device except to sync through your own iCloud.
What does Pro unlock?
The free version holds up to three watches. A single one-time Pro purchase unlocks unlimited watches and removes the small watermark from PDF and CSV exports. It's a one-time purchase — never a subscription.
I bought Pro on another device / reinstalled. How do I get it back?
Open Settings and tap “Restore purchase.” As long as you use the same Apple Account, your Pro unlock returns at no extra cost.
How do I export an insurance report?
Settings → Export → Insurance report (PDF). You'll get a cover page with your total collection value plus one page per watch, ready to share with your insurer.
How do service reminders work?
When you log a service on a watch, you can set a “next service due” date. WristVault schedules a local reminder on your device for that day.
