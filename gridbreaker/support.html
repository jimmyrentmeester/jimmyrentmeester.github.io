Quick help

How do I play? First launch runs an interactive training. You can replay it anytime from Settings ▸ How to Play.

What are Credits (CR) for? Every run earns CR. Spend them in the Cyberdeck on permanent upgrades and on Cosmetics (palettes & tap-trails).

Red tiles? Those are firewalls — never tap them; let them expire safely.

Sound, haptics, motion? Toggle sound/haptics and set music & effects volume in Settings. The game also respects iOS Reduce Motion.

Reset my progress? Settings ▸ Reset Progress (asks to confirm). This clears scores, credits, upgrades and cosmetics; preferences are kept.

Is my data private? Yes — nothing is collected or sent. See the Privacy Policy.
