// privacy policy

GRID_BREAKER does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking.

What the app stores

The game saves your settings, progress, high scores, credits, and cosmetics locally on your device only, using Apple’s standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is removed when you delete the app.

What we don’t do

  • No personal information is collected or requested.
  • No network connections, servers, or cloud services.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.
  • No access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photos.
  • No tracking across apps or websites.

Children

The app is safe for all ages with respect to privacy: because no data is collected, nothing about any user — including children — is gathered or shared.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
