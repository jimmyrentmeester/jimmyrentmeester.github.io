GRID_BREAKER

// privacy policy

GRID_BREAKER has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising and no tracking. I do not collect, receive or store any data about you. There is one thing that leaves your device, and only if you choose it: Game Center. It is explained below.

What the app stores

The game saves your settings, progress, high scores, credits, and cosmetics locally on your device only, using Apple’s standard on-device storage (UserDefaults). This data is removed when you delete the app.

Game Center

GRID_BREAKER uses Apple’s Game Center for global leaderboards and achievements. If you are signed in to Game Center on your device, your scores and achievement progress are sent to Apple, and your Game Center nickname appears on the public leaderboards next to your score. That is the whole exchange.

This is handled entirely by Apple and governed by Apple’s privacy policy. I have no server involved, I receive nothing, and I see no more than any other player looking at the same public leaderboard. If you are not signed in to Game Center, or you turn it off in iOS Settings, the game plays exactly the same and nothing is sent anywhere.

What I don’t do

  • No personal information is collected or requested.
  • No servers of my own — there is no GRID_BREAKER account and no backend.
  • No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs.
  • No access to camera, microphone, location, contacts, or photos.
  • No tracking across apps or websites.

Children

The app collects nothing about anyone, children included. Note that Game Center is Apple’s service: parents can restrict or disable it under iOS Screen Time.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.
