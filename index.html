How I work

  • Ship it, then judge it A finished version one beats a perfect plan. Everything I have learned about these apps, I learned after release.
  • Cut until it is clear Most features are deferred, not rejected. A first version that explains itself is worth more than a complete one that does not.
  • Private by default No accounts, no ads, no tracking, in every app. It rules out easy growth tactics, which is precisely what makes it a promise.
  • Own the whole path Research, design, code, store listing, pricing, localisation, tax and compliance. Knowing what it takes to release changes what you decide to build.
